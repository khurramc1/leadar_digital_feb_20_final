How We Build Your Lead Generation System

A proven, transparent process designed specifically for B2B service companies. No black boxes. No proprietary secrets. Just systematic execution that generates results.

The 4-Phase Framework

Most agencies promise "results in 30 days" then disappear when it doesn't happen. We're different. Here's the honest truth:

Phase 1 (Months 1-2)

Foundation building. Minimal leads, lots of setup.

Phase 2 (Months 3-4)

Early traction. Leads increasing, campaigns optimizing.

Phase 3 (Months 5-6)

Momentum building. Consistent lead flow emerging.

Phase 4 (Months 7-12+)

Established pipeline. Scalable, predictable system.

Total time to full ROI: 6-9 months

Anyone promising faster is either running unsustainable paid-only campaigns, defining "results" differently than you, or lying. We build sustainable systems that compound over time.

1

Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-2)

Week 1-2: Discovery & Strategy

What We Do:

  • Strategy Workshop (2-3 hour session) - understand your business model, services, and goals
  • Identify your ideal client profile
  • Review current marketing efforts and results
  • Market Research - analyze 10-15 direct competitors
  • Research search volumes and keyword difficulty
  • Map buyer journey for your services

Deliverable: Comprehensive strategy document (30-40 pages) including target keywords, content calendar (first 3 months), channel strategy and budget allocation, competitive positioning recommendations, and success metrics.

Week 3-4: Foundation Building

What We Do:

  • Technical SEO Audit & Fixes (site speed, mobile responsiveness, schema markup)
  • Analytics Setup (Google Analytics 4, conversion tracking, custom dashboards)
  • Website Optimization (service pages, lead magnets, contact forms)

Week 5-8: Launch & Monitor

  • First 6-8 blog posts published
  • Google Ads campaigns go live
  • Email sequences activated
  • Social media posting begins

Expected Results: 2-8 qualified leads (mostly from paid ads), SEO improvements beginning, analytics operational

2

Phase 2: Early Traction (Months 3-4)

What We Do:

  • Content Expansion (4-8 additional articles)
  • Ad Optimization (pause underperforming keywords, increase bids on high-converters)
  • SEO Progress (monitor rankings, build backlinks)
  • LinkedIn Activation (increase posting, launch LinkedIn Ads)
  • Conversion Optimization (A/B test landing pages, optimize forms)

Expected Results: 8-18 qualified leads/month, organic traffic up 60-100%, ad campaigns optimized (cost per lead decreased 20-30%)

3

Phase 3: Momentum (Months 5-6)

What We Do:

  • Content Authority (24-40+ total articles published)
  • Aggressive Optimization (double down on winning keywords, expand best campaigns)
  • SEO Expansion (target additional keyword clusters, build strategic backlinks)
  • Lead Nurturing Enhancement (segment leads, personalized sequences)

Expected Results: 15-30 qualified leads/month, first page rankings for 15-25 keywords, organic traffic up 200-300%, ROI positive (2-5x)

4

Phase 4: Established & Scaling (Months 7-12+)

What We Do:

  • Content Compounding (continue 4-12 articles/month, update top performers)
  • Authority Building (speaking opportunities, media features, partnerships)
  • Advanced Tactics (account-based marketing, sophisticated remarketing)
  • Scaling (expand to new services/markets, increase ad budgets on proven campaigns)

Expected Results: 25-50+ qualified leads/month, organic driving 50-60% of leads, strong ROI (6-15x), sustainable scalable system

What Makes Our Process Different

Industry-Specific from Day 1

We don't apply generic templates. Property management campaigns target MCST council members during AGM season. Recruitment agency campaigns target corporate HR managers. Same channels, completely different strategies.

Multi-Channel Integration

SEO builds long-term authority. Paid Ads generate immediate visibility. Content educates prospects. Email nurtures leads. LinkedIn reaches decision-makers. Together, they create a resilient system.

Transparent Reporting

You always know: how many leads you got, where they came from, what they cost, which converted to clients, and what your ROI is. Real-time dashboard access, monthly detailed reports, clear attribution.

You Own Everything

Website improvements, all content, ad accounts, email lists, analytics setup, all creative assets. If we part ways, you take it all. No proprietary systems holding your data hostage.
